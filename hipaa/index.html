Most LA medical and dental practices assume their EHR vendor's security covers them for HIPAA. It doesn't. The Security Risk Analysis is a legal requirement under the HIPAA Security Rule, and it has to cover your whole practice: staff training, device encryption, access controls, backups, and breach response.

This checklist tells you exactly where practices your size usually fall short, before OCR, a denied insurance claim, or ransomware finds the gap for you.

What's inside

  • Administrative controls: designated security officer, written policies, signed Business Associate Agreements
  • Technical controls: MFA, encryption, unique logins, tested backups
  • Physical and response controls: device security, written breach-response plan
