Privacy Policy

How Secure Roots collects, uses, and protects your information

Effective date: June 14, 2026

Secure Roots, LLC (“Secure Roots,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the website secureroots.io (the “Site”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that you choose to provide and a limited amount of technical information needed to operate the Site.

  • Information you provide directly. When you submit our contact form or subscribe to our newsletter, we collect the information you enter — such as your name, email address, and any message you send us.
  • Information collected automatically. Like most websites, our hosting provider automatically records standard technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit. This is used for security, reliability, and basic operation of the Site.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request.
  • To send you newsletters and updates that you have subscribed to.
  • To operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Site.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share information only as needed to operate the Site and only with the service providers described below, or when required by law.

Third-Party Services

We rely on a small number of trusted third-party providers to run the Site. Each handles your information under its own privacy policy:

  • GitHub Pages — hosts the Site and processes standard server request data.
  • FormSubmit — processes contact-form and newsletter submissions and delivers them to us by email.

Cookies and Tracking

The Site does not set advertising or analytics cookies of its own. Third-party providers may use limited cookies or similar technologies that are strictly necessary to deliver their services. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Retention

We keep the information you provide only as long as needed to respond to you, to provide the newsletter while you remain subscribed, and to meet our legal and operational requirements. You may ask us to delete your information at any time.

Your Choices and Rights

  • Newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email or by contacting us.
  • Access and deletion. You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to correct or delete it, by emailing us.
  • California residents. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request its deletion, and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. We do not sell personal information. To make a request, contact us at the email below.

Data Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

The Site is intended for a general, professional audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Effective date” above. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
